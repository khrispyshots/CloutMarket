# Project Brief: CloutMarket — SocialFi on Celo

## 1. Product Vision
CloutMarket is a mobile-first Social Finance (SocialFi) platform designed to bridge the gap between social influence and financial investment. By allowing users to invest in their favorite creators, CloutMarket creates a symbiotic ecosystem where fans profit from a creator's growth and creators gain direct financial support from their community.

**Tagline:** "Invest in people before the world does."

## 2. Target Audience
- **Gen Z & Millennial Social Media Users:** Digitally native individuals comfortable with the creator economy.
- **Non-Crypto Natives:** Users looking for simple investment opportunities without the complexity of traditional DeFi.
- **Creators:** Influencers, artists, and builders seeking new ways to monetize their brand and engage their most loyal followers.

## 3. Design Philosophy (Soft Retro Play)
The visual identity is intentionally non-technical to lower the barrier to entry for SocialFi.
- **Aesthetic:** Playful, cozy, and friendly. A "vintage mobile UI" feel.
- **Core Elements:** Thick 2px borders, large 24px corner radiuses, and "sticker-like" cards.
- **Spacing:** High-density whitespace and generous padding (24px) to ensure the UI "breathes."
- **Color Palette:** Warm off-white backgrounds with soft pastel blocks (Celo Green, Soft Yellow, Light Blue).

## 4. Key Features & User Journey

### A. Onboarding & Security
- **Playful Education:** A 3-step visual onboarding flow explaining the "Invest & Support" model.
- **Mobile-First Auth:** Simple social login or email options, leading into wallet connection.
- **Celo Integration:** Deep integration with Celo-native wallets like Valora for low-fee, instant transactions.

### B. Discovery & Investment
- **Home Feed:** A curated feed of trending and new creators with high-impact "Invest" actions.
- **Creator Profiles:** Social profiles that display market stats (Market Cap, Token Price) alongside social content.
- **Bonding Curve Trading:** Simplified "Buy/Sell" mechanism where users trade creator tokens directly against a curve, ensuring liquidity.

### C. Portfolio Management
- **Visual Performance:** A simplified line chart and performance summary.
- **Asset Overview:** Card-based list of holdings showing value and growth percentage.

## 5. Technical Requirements (Celo Network)
- **Platform:** Mobile-first web application.
- **Blockchain:** Celo (Layer 1) for high speed and sub-cent gas fees.
- **Assets:** CELO (Gas/Native), cUSD (Stablecoin), and Creator Tokens (Custom ERC-20).

## 6. UX Rules
- **No Jargon:** Use "Invest" instead of "Stake," and "Support" instead of "Provide Liquidity."
- **Clarity over Complexity:** Avoid dense financial tables or complex candlestick charts.
- **Instant Feedback:** Use playful animations and clear success states for all transactions.
